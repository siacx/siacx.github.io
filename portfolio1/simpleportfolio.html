About Me
Thanks for your interest, here is a quick story of me and this website.
My artistic journey began on a magical Christmas Day when my brother and I received a PS2 along with the game Resident Evil, a classic that my father insisted we play. Little did I know, this gift would ignite my imagination and inspire me to embark on a journey of my own—one that involves creating captivating worlds filled with unique characters and stories. With every brushstroke and digital render, I aim to bring my visions to life, combining elements of fantasy and adventure that resonate with fellow gamers and art enthusiasts alike.

Join me as I explore new realms and share my passion for illustration!


- MoniiQ.

Hi, I'm Asia Ceballos

I'm a passionate artist and aspiring concept designer. I specialize in creating unique worlds and characters through illustration and animation. I have always been fascinated by video games and how their visual storytelling can transport people to different realities. My goal is to work in the entertainment industry, specifically in video games and animation, as a character designer or concept artist.

My Journey

My artistic journey began when I was gifted a PlayStation 2 and the game Resident Evil, which inspired me to create my own worlds and characters. Since then, I've been exploring various mediums and techniques to improve my skills and bring my visions to life.
